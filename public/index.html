What are the small plates?

Nevertheless, I have received a commandment of the Lord that I should make these plates, for the special purpose that there should be an account engraven of the ministry of my people. (1 Nephi 3:9)

Oh you meant this app? Just as the small plates of Nephi were a record kept and shared by his family while the large plates were kept by the rulers of the people, so will your small plates be a record of your life meant for you and your closests family and friends.
